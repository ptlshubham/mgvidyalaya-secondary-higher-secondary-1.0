Mahatma Gandhi Vidhyalaya, Mogari (Secondary and Higher Secondary)

Established in 1966, our school has been a pillar of excellence in education, shaping young minds with knowledge, values, and skills for a bright future. Nestled in a clean and natural environment, our campus provides a peaceful and stimulating setting where students can thrive academically and personally. Our school is committed to providing quality education through a team of highly skilled and experienced teachers who ensure that learning is engaging, interactive, and comprehensive. To enhance the learning experience, each classroom is equipped with the latest smart TVs, allowing teachers to integrate modern technology into their lessons effectively.
